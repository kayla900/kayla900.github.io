LEMON COOKIES

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 lemon
  • 1.5 cups sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Combine dry ingredients and then add wet ingredient. Add lemon zest.
  3. Roll into balls approximately 1 inch in diameter. Place on cookie sheet.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.

BLACK BEAN SOUP

Ingredients

  • 1 tb olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 stalk celery
  • 2 carrots
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tb cumin
  • black pepper to taste
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 4 cans of black beans
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes

Directions

  1. Heat oil, add onion, celery, carrots and garlic. Season with cumin, chili powder, and pepper. Pour in vegetable broth, 2 cans of beans, and corn. Boil.
  2. Blend other 2 cans of beans in food processor with tomatoes. Stir into soup mixture and reduce heat. Simmer for 15 minutes.
